The problem: context & objectives

Airlines operating globally face important challenges in relation to getting paid.

Firstly, acquirers (banks of each airline) consider their business as a high risk industry, mainly because the low margins and the fact of ticket sales happening in advance (before customers enjoy flight services).

Secondly, the international implications of their business compel them to engage a significant amount of providers (bank at headquarters, other local acquirers with different regulations, local specifities, fraud, foreign exchange, PSPs, different methods of payment, etc.).

As a results, payments management by airlines meet the following drawbacks:

  • Lack of flexibility, difficult adaptability to changing environment.
  • Lack of global view of business.
  • Lack of negotiation power in non-core/new markets.
  • Hard reconciliation processes.
  • Need of local teams, making more complex an already complex back office.

Benefits of acquiring orchestration and complexity

Amadeus and its payment product portfolio are in a position to offer solutions that at least mitigate these airline problems (lower acquiring fees, enhanced settlement periods, more knowledge with BI dashboards, virtual acquiring, etc.). However, the complexity of the solution is high: it needs to orchestrate an assortment of systems, processes and communications with partners that often have challenging specificities.

For instance, when liaising with a partner PSP:

  • There is a strong dependency with the automatic and manual processes of the partner.
  • It can name, organize or model data following an approach different to those used by our systems or other partners.
  • Complex and heterogeneus logic might be required because many processes are done with batch jobs that produce files containing transaction records, utilizing cross-references to relate those associated.
  • It can support a reduced set of data or a reduced set of process variants (for instance, the capture can be done online, offline, triggered by some file, ... and the chargeback can offer only some chargeback stages).

In addition, specialized modules and partners are required to deal with other features like fraud detection, foreign exchange setups or reconciliation (reconciliation consists on ensuring there is a confirmed payment transaction for every sale).
